About Me

This is about Cherry.

Bing L. Cherry is, first and foremost, a Christian. While he doesn't claim to know everything nor have all the answers, he hopes that, through his website, he can share some useful food for thought.

Cherry was born in Minnesota, grew up in California, and worked in Taiwan and Japan for many years before moving back to California. He's currently living with his partner and a sizable collection of plushies.
