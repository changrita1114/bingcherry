Respect People, not Beliefs

  • February 27, 2021
  • Opinion
  • Bing L. Cherry

First, to make things clear, I am not claiming that people should be disrespected or mistreated because of their beliefs. Nor am I claiming that my beliefs will always be superior, or that a certain set of beliefs must absolutely be the 'correct way'. What I will say is that beliefs drive our very words and actions, and thus, we should always examine these beliefs to ensure that we have a solid foundation that makes our lives, and hopefully society as a whole, a better place to live in.
