Global Blessing

Founded by Minnie Li, 'Global Blessing' is a consulting agency which aims to help rebuild Fukushima Prefecture in Japan through a non-profit organisation. Fukushima translated literally is 'Island of Blessing', and indeed Fukushima is a place full of blessings. Minnie came from Hong Kong and volunteered in Japan and other countries for many years. On the 10th anniversary of the Great East Japan earthquake, she started 'Global Blessing' with her friends. They hope to continue to work with local and foreign volunteers, organizations and individuals to support Fukushima. Furthermore, they hope that the blessing from Fukushima will be extended to other places in Japan and abroad. This would bring the meaning of Fukushima's name to life.
